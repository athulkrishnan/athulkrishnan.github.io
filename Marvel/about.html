Who we are

Marvel Structural Consultants is a design and structural consultancy firm based in Chennai, started by alumini of IIT Madras. Marvel focuses on structural design solutions including concrete and steel detailing services and building design services. We have a team of professional structural engineers and draftsmen familiar with international steel/concrete design and detailing standards and codes. We provide steel and concrete detailing services for all types of high rise and low rise residential and commercial buildings. We have successfully completed several structural design and detailing projects till now, the details of which can be found in the projects section.
