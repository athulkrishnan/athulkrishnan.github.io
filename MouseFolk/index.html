Our Philosophy

Here at MouseFolk, we believe in the inherent power of the paintbrush, that intricate musical note, and the written word. Being artists, we know the joy of bringing fragile products of the mind to tactile, audial and shareable forms of the human condition. The joy of creativity is unlike anything else.

The path to creating, we have experienced, is hard. We're here to make it a little simpler for people like us. We'll ensure that your work of art is appreciated by the world - as much as the joy you had in creating it. Our vision is to make the artistic experience a bit more simpler, a bit more rewarding, and a lot more fulfilling.

In case you were wondering, our name is inspired by Franz Kafka's final short story - “Josephine the Singer, or The Mouse Folk”. We identified with it, for it narrates the plight of a singer, who happens to be a mouse. It best describes the artistic experience, of standing by your vision even when predicaments say otherwise.

Here are some queries we get frequently.
Where will my artwork be used?
You have full control over how your artwork will be used. Your work can be provided to buyers as printed t-shirts, posters, or as digital downloads.
What about copyrights?
You retain all rights over your work, we only license them.
How do I get rewarded?
You'll be paid for sales within 4-6 days. Cheque, bank transfer or online wallets - you name it.
What are the membership charges?
None. We charge a commision of 20-40 percent on each sale, excluding the printing and shipping charges.
