How it Works?

The incredibly simpler way to monetize your art

Apply

To get started, apply to join our platform. Once on-board, share your best creations with us

Curation

Our curator examines your work and the ones that pass our benchmarks are made available for purchase

Sales

Your work will be made available for sale in a variety of forms. You control how it is sold

Royalties

You receive a payout for the sale by 7 working days. Cheque, bank transfer, e-wallet - you name it

Sales and Licensing

We are building our marketplace for artwork and have partnered with a number of brands that will make selling your art a breeze. Our in-house brands will use artwork for use on t-shirts and apparel. Needless to say, you have total control where and how your artwork is used.

Our Philosophy

Here at MouseFolk, we believe in the inherent power of the paintbrush, that intricate musical note, and the written word. Being artists, we know the joy of bringing fragile, evolving ideas of the mind into visual, tactile and shareable forms.

The joy of creativity is unlike anything else. The path to creativity though, is quite demanding. We're here to make it a little simpler for artists like you. We'll ensure that your creativity is appreciated by the world - as much as the joy you had in creating it. Our vision is to make the artistic experience a little less painful, a bit more rewarding, and a lot more fulfilling. You keep making great art, we’ll make sure you find the right audience.
