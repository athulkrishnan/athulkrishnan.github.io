Collaborate

Tvasta Manufacturing Solutions (a startup launched by IIT Madras Alumni) and IIT-Madras have been jointly pursuing the goal of building a solution for employing 3D Printing in Construction. We are currently jointly establishing relationship with Industry Partners and Researchers across the globe in pursuit of this goal.

The goal of this venture is to build a complete solution for 3D Printing in Construction. We aim to make the construction process faster, simpler and more versatile by applying the principles of 3D Printing in Construction buildings. We aim to employ this technology in making the Construction process affordable for mass construction. The vision is also to build versatility into the construction process whereby complex architectural shapes could be built with ease using 3D Printing.

The Research Group is a multi-disciplined team pursuing research in several areas to make a complete technological solution viable. Research is being attempted in the area of 3D Printing Process, Material Development, Algorithms for Path Planning and Construction Automation.

Scope for Research and Development

Machine Design

The Research Group is currently pursuing development of customized Concrete Mixes for 3D Printing process. The Concrete mixes developed are aimed to address the several changes that the construction process undergoes when 3D Printing is applied. The strengths of the 3D Printing process need to be leveraged and the shortcomings of the process addressed with this customized mix design.
